JS Date Format

      The ISO format follows a strict standards in JS. 
      The other formats are not so well defined and night be  browser specific.

JS Date Output

Indepedent of input format, JS will (by default) output dates in full tect strinf format.

Tue Feb 27 2024 20:22:52 GMT+0000 (Greenwich Mean Time)

JS ISO Dates

      ISO 8601 is the international standard for the represantation of dates times.
      The ISO 8601 syntax is also the preferred JS date format.

const d = new Date("2015-03-25") ;

Wed Mar 25 2015 00:00:00 GMT+0000 (Greenwich Mean Time)

ISO Dates (Year and Month)

ISO dates can be written without specifying the day( YYYY - MM ):

const d = new Date ("2015-03") ;

Sun Mar 01 2015 00:00:00 GMT+0000 (Greenwich Mean Time)

ISO dates (Year Only)

ISO dates can be written without month and day (YYYY):

const d = new Date("2015") ;

Thu Jan 01 2015 00:00:00 GMT+0000 (Greenwich Mean Time)
